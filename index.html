Frequently Asked Questions

How does the vocabulary lesson work?

Click any lesson button to load words. Each word card shows meaning, pronunciation, and you can click info or listen to pronunciation.

Can I search any word?

Yes, the search bar filters vocabulary across all lessons. It updates in real time with matching words.

Is the pronunciation feature free?

Absolutely! Click the speaker icon to hear the word spoken using browser's speech synthesis.
